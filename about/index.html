About Me

glkwhr is just some random letters followed by my initials, in order to make a “unique” username, which I’ve been using for over 10 years. It has nothing to do with Benz, just in case you are wondering.

I’m now studying as a graduate Computer Science student at New York University, Tandon School of Engineering, graduating in May 2018.

Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2016.

About My Blog

The purpose of this blog is pretty simple, that is to serve as a notebook of mine. I keep running into various issues and problems during the process of studying, either technical or academical. It would have been a much harder time for me without the articles people wrote to explain the techniques or concepts. Those articles can be enlightening, even if not directly solve my problems.

That’s why I decided to start my own blog as well, not only to keep track of learning, but also to hopefully provide useful information to others. So, please feel free to comment if you have any question or suggestion.
